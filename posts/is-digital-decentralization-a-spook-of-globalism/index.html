Is Digital Decentralization a spook of Globalism?

20 Mar 2021 | 1 min read

Well, shortly I am not sure about that “life-like” decentralization is a spook. It could be a spreading idea on internet boards and totally harmless for society, I’m talking about digital-decentralization, it is connected to main decentralization idea but I’m not an expert of other branches like statistics and sociology stuff so I’m only talking about digital results of decentralization. Other side of the coin I’m absolutely a decentralization supporter. In my opinion, localizm is for our favour.

Relationship of a benefit keeps people together. This benefit measured with a common system and we call it money. Money prints in mints and release by the governments and they all connected to FED. This thing that all people use is arranges, rules and manages by a group of people, isn’t this wrong? As for me, this is a huge issue. There were other people who thought like me and they invented blockchain. (or I/we realized this problem after the invention of blockchain.)

Anyway, let’s get to the point. Globalism rules this era and they have all power like media, techs, manpower and they have the main power, internet. They’re trying to spy and log us by using NSA. (even as I write this) but if there is no money, they can’t rule and blockchain is taking this power from their hands. Frankly, I have doubts that the blockchain is not decentralized because they are not reacting well against it.

I think reserves of all cryptocurrencies are manage like the FED’s. Cryptologists and mathematicians may disagree with what I say, these are just my own observations.
