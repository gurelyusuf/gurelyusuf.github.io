YouTube Channels I like watching

14 Mar 2021 | 1 min read

All these channels is about CS/IT. If you like to watch some beep-boop on terminals & boards, you’ll like all of them. I’ll update it regularly.

‏
  • ITTFami – Old electronics, low production value, occasional swearing, and a tad of madness, go ahead and watch his crap.
‏
  • tripcode!Q/7 – If you scrolled on /g/ board, you’ve definitely seen a meme about him (Yeas, him) and his channel is generally about ThinkPad hardware and bootloaders/BIOS.
‏
  • Luke Smith – Well, He’s an edgelord for all those GNU stuff but he also makes videos about philosophy and the nonsense of modern life and digital globalism. This boomer gonna redpilled you.
‏
  • WesleyAda – A channel about introduction to GNU/Linux, you can take a look if you’re a Linux noob.
‏
  • sentdex – I really like and envy his videos. He is making about ML, finance, data analysis, robotics, web development, game development.
‏
  • Druaga1 – Do you wanne see extreme experiments on old techs? Watch this channel.
‏
  • PhilsComputerLab – Tests, benchmarks, comparisons on antique hardwares.
‏
  • Wolfgang’s Channel – He has the best tutorials & ASMRs about installations. I built Gentoo with him at first time.
‏
  • Mental Outlaw – An another my favorite channel. He’s making videos about these sub-cultures and how-to videos for Gentoo and other distros.
‏
  • george hotz archive – If you don’t know George Hotz, I suggest you research him. He’s like a villain against Apple. That channel includes his programming streams and other videos.
‏
  • DenshiVideo – He makes videos about software, operating systems, and pretty much anything else.
‏
