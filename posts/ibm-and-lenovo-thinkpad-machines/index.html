I’m gonna tell about reasons to buy ThinkPad. If you are working on bootloaders, BIOS as System Programmer probably you have one of these machines because of Libreboot, coreboot and totally GNU/Linux & BSD support also you are working on basic programming, the best mobile machine to buy is a ThinkPad.

Before starting, if you’re NOT interested in majors of Computer Science and you’re a compuer normie, ThinkPads will become DumbPad for you.

Why ThinkPad?

  • Used machines are plentiful and cheap (even for Turkey)
  • Excellent keyboards – tactile feel and quiet
  • Great durability: magnesium rollcage for structural integrity, high quality plastic body panels
  • Utilitarian design: indicator LEDs, 7 row keyboard layout on older models
  • Docking stations that easily turns your laptop into a desktop
  • Easy to repair (most models), upgrade & maintain thanks to readily available service manuals for every model, spare parts easy & cheap to obtain
  • The TrackPoint™, great for those who type a lot or hate people swiping their fingers all over a touchpad
  • Excellent GNU/Linux & BSD support
